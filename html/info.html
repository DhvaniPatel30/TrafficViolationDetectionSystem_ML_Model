Traffic Rules and Safety

Understanding traffic rules and practicing road safety is essential for everyone on the road. Here are some important tips:

  • Always wear a helmet when riding a motorcycle.
  • Obey speed limits and road signs.
  • Keep a safe following distance from the vehicle in front of you.
  • Use turn signals to indicate your intentions.
